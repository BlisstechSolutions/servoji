Blisstech Solutions — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Blissstech Solutions Private Limited ("Blisstech," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and shares personal data when you use our mobile application ("App") and associated services (collectively, "Services").

By accessing or using our Services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Scope and Applicability

This Policy applies to both:

  • Service Customers: Individuals who use our App to book or receive services.
  • Service Professionals: Individuals who register on our App to offer and provide services to Service Customers.

3. The Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide to Us

For all users:

  • Registration Data: Your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile Information: Photo, professional details, and other data you add to your profile.

For Service Customers:

  • Service location, preferences, and booking details.
  • Billing info, including name, address, and payment details (processed securely via third-party gateways like Razorpay).

For Service Professionals:

  • Verification info (PAN, Aadhaar, registration, and bank details).
  • Background check data via trusted third-party partners.
  • Service expertise, portfolio, and credentials.

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Location Data: We collect your location when using the App for matching customers and professionals.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, bookings made, and time spent on the App.
  • Device Data: Device model, OS version, and mobile network info.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tools to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our Services.
  • To match customers with professionals efficiently.
  • To process payments and issue refunds.
  • To send notifications, updates, and promotional messages (if opted).
  • To prevent fraud and ensure user safety.
  • To comply with Indian legal obligations.

5. How We Share Your Information

  • With the other party involved in a service (Customer ↔ Professional).
  • With trusted third-party service providers for cloud, analytics, payments, and verification.
  • With legal authorities when required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your data anytime via your profile.
  • Disable location tracking (may affect functionality).
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime.
  • Request complete data deletion by contacting us at support@blisstechsolutions.in.

Data Deletion Request: We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and delete your data within 7 business days.

7. Data Security

We employ encryption, access control, and monitoring to secure your personal data. While we strive for complete protection, no system is entirely immune from breaches.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy occasionally. Any major changes will be announced via the App and this page, along with an updated effective date.
